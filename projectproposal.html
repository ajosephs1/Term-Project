Barter

What is your Website about?

This website will be a trading website that will allow Babson students or nearby college students/ youth to exchage items or "Barter credit" for other items such as furniture clothing or books.

What is the objective of your website?

The object of this website is to have a page where students can easily find items in a social college community rather than having to look for more expensive alternatives online. This will allow for more for convenience for the college shopper as they could socialize with possible traders to assure quality. Mitigate the amount of waste usually seen at the end of the year as people move back home.

Who is your target audience

My target audienced is mainly college youth residing on Babson Campus or nearby universities but could also include short term tenants or people in nearby neighbourhoods who think they have something valuable to offer to the community.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

  • Page 1: Market place where people can explore to purchase items with Barter credits or item exchange
  • Page 2: Message center where people can manage offers and make trades
  • Page 3: Profile page where a trader can manage their profiles with contact, info, picture etc
  • Page 4: About page and Barter etiquette/rules
